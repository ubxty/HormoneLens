Powerful Features for Better Health

Predictive Simulation

Input any meal or activity and get instant predictions of glucose and cortisol spikes (High/Medium/Low risk) before you act. Make informed decisions in real-time.

Context-Aware AI

Our RAG-powered system retrieves your historical health logs to personalize predictions. The AI learns that "dairy causes bloating" for you specifically.

Safety Guardrails

AWS Bedrock Guardrails filter out unsafe medical advice automatically. Your safety is our priority with built-in content moderation.

Metabolic Score

Multi-Model Validation

Mobile Responsive

Data Privacy

Lightning Fast
